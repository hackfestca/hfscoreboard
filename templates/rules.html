General Informations

Intro
Welcome to Hackfest 2014 CTF. This year’s 16 hour King of the Hill CTF was inspired by last year’s War Game and classical CTF. We hope you’ll enjoy!
Context
The year is 2338. The planet is filthy and overpopulated. Governments have been replaced by large self-preserving multinational companies.
You are hired by a multinational company to break into family business Tessier-Ashpool. Acquire all access codes, discover every secret. However, several other enterprises also want the information contained in these databanks and they have all hired expert hackers to help.

Your tasks

Your tasks is to complete the different challenge. You will find all challenge on the scoreboard. Once you complete a challenge, you’ll find a flag which can be submitted.
There are two types of flag :
regular flag
  • Flags are obtained by solving challenges.
  • They can be submitted once.
  • Format is: FLAG-[a-zA-Z0-9]{32,60}
KOTH flag
  • King of the Hill flags (KOTH flags) are obtained by maintaining access to a KOTH challenge.
  • These challenges are not on the scoreboard and can be found from other challenges.
  • Format is: KOTH-[a-zA-Z0-9]{32}
Flags must be submitted without the prefix FLAG or KOTH.

Scoring system

URL : http://scoreboard.hf
You can submit your flag directly on the scoreboard or download the command line script to push your flag and more https://scoreboard.hf/public/player.zip
